Where do you stand?


Need help deciding?

Election day is approaching quickly, yet many voters are struggling to decide which ballot to cast when the day comes. Making an educated vote has always been a challenging task, but the spontaneity of a snap election and the chaos of the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic make the political climate in Canada even more difficult to comprehend. With this in mind, we created a quiz that can help voters determine which parties their political views align with the most. However, it must be noted that the results of a short quiz cannot compare to the value of informing oneself through in-depth research and discussion. After taking the quiz, we encourage you to follow the links below in order to learn more about the major parties and their values.
